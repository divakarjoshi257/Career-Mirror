Privacy Policy for Career Mirror

Effective Date: 13 February 2026

Career Mirror ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited, non-personally identifiable information for analytics purposes:

  • Years of experience (categorized)
  • Career field
  • Company type
  • Career goal
  • Salary range (categorized)
  • App usage data

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identity information
  • Exact salary details
  • Sensitive personal data

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to improve app functionality and enhance AI-powered career insights.

3. Data Storage

Anonymous analytics data may be stored securely using Firebase services. We do not sell or share your data.

4. AI Processing

Career input data may be processed by third-party AI services to generate reports. No personal identifying information is stored.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.
